Goverment Jobs after Class 10

  • SSC MTS
    •

    SSC MTS is a great opportunity for the 10th pass candidates to get a government job in India. SSC has announced to release the official notification for SSC MTS 2022 Examination on 22nd March 2022 and will carry the online application process from 22nd March to 30th April 2022

  • RRB Group D

    • RRB Group D Admit Card 2022 for Phase 1 Exam is going to release on 15 March 2022. Railway Recruitment Board is going to release RRB Group D Hall Ticket 2022 on their regional recruitment portal

  • RPF Constable

    • Indian Railway Protection Force (RPF) and Railway Protection Special Force (RPSF) are recruiting male and female police officers through the RPF Constable Recruitment 2022 Exam. The Ministry of Railways notifies an RPF Constable to fill a large number of positions in India in Railway areas

Goverment Jobs after class 12

  • SSC CHSL

    • SSC CHSL is a combined competition exam conducted to select Higher Secondary qualified students into various departments and offices of the government. The full form of SSC CHSL is the Staff Selection Commission Combined Higher Secondary Level Exam. SSC CHSL (10+2) includes: Lower Division Clerk.

  • SSC General Duty Constable (SSC GD Exam)

    • SSC GD Constable Exam is being conducted by the Staff Selection Commission of India to recruit candidates for the General Duty post of Constables (GD) in BSF, CISF, ITBP, CRPF and Rifleman in AR. SSC GD Constable 2021 Notification has been released by the Commission to recruit 25271 posts of constables for both male and female.

  • SSC Grade C and Grade D Stenographer (SSC Stenographer)

    • The SSC Stenographer exam consists of a written test and an SSC Stenographer Skill Test. The written test consists of 200 objective type questions for 200 marks to be finished in 2 hours. In pre-exam questions from Reasoning, General awareness and English sections will be asked.

  • RRB Assistant Loco Pilot (RRB ALP)

    • Railway Recruitment Boards (RRBs) conduct RRB ALP exam to recruit Assistant Loco Pilot and Technician. RRB ALP notification is expected to be released in November or December 2022. RRB ALP notification was last released in 2018 wherein a total of 64,371 vacancies were announced. A total of 47.45 lakh candidates had applied for the exam in 2018.

  • NAVY Secondary Recruits (SSR)

    • The Indian Navy SSR AA recruitment 2022 notification PDF is now released officially. Candidates who want to join the Navy and serve the nation will greatly benefit from Indian Navy Sailor recruitment 2022 which happens annually
