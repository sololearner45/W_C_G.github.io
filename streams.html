Career Options after class 10


The most important decision after class 10 is opting for the right stream of study. Selecting the right stream is really important as your future depends on it. Stay away from the herd mentality of going for the course your friends are enrolling for or the one your parents are suggesting. This erroneous and perilous practice often leads to faulty career choices as in doing so you opt for the stream without actually understanding what lies in store after joining it


The different streams that are available after Class 10 are:

  1. Science

    2. Science has been the most opted for stream.

    The major advantage of science is, after class 12th you can switch from science to commerce or science to arts. There are multiple career options available for the science stream after class 12th.

    There are two option in Science

    1. PCM

      2. In Pcm the main subjects are Physics Chemistry and math

      Career option for Pcm student

      • B-tech/BE
      • Defence Services
      • Merchent Navy
    2. PCB

      3. Main Subject in PCB are physics chemistry and biology.

      Carriear option for PCB student

      • Bachelor of Medicine & Bachelor of Surgery ( MBBS)
      • Bachelor of Pharmacy
      • BAMS

  2. Commerece

    3. Commerce is the second most popular career option after science. Commerce is best for Business. If you are fascinated by numbers, finances, and economics then commerce is for you.

    Main subject in Commerece stream are Accounts, Economics and Bussiness

    Career options for Commerece student :-

    • Chartered Accountant
    • Company Secretary
    • Business Management

  3. Arts/ Humanities

    4. Arts/ Humanities are of those who are interested in academic research. If you are creative and want to dive deep into Humanity, then arts is the stream for you

    The core subjects offered in Arts are Sociology, History, Literature, Political Science, Psychology, Economics, Philosophy, Fine Arts, etc along with one compulsory language subject.

    Career Options for Arts Stream Student :-

    • Media / Journalism
    • Product Designing
    • Civil Service Exams

  4. Polytechnic courses

    5. After class 10th, students can go for polytechnic courses like Mechanical, Civil, Chemical, Computer, Automobile

    Career Options for Polytechnic courses

    • Private jobs
    • Goverment Jobs
    • Own Bussiness
